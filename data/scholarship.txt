Tech Courses ) Note on Institute Scholarships: All the percentage is overall aggregate mentioned above are based on the candidate’s 10+2 (Class 12th) Board results for UG course admission & qualifying exam for Lateral / PG Course admissions. For JEE Scholarships, candidate must furnish the current year Common Rank List JEE Mains. For National Entrance Scholarships, candidate must furnish the current year rank card of CAT / MAT / XAT / CMAT / CUET. All the percentage / CGPA mentioned above are based on the candidate’s undergraduate results for all PG course admissions. The candidate should provide Class 12th board / UG passing certificate (pass in all the subjects) to avail any scholarship scheme in SMIT. Candidates can avail only one type of scholarship. Scholarship schemes are applicable for the entire duration of their study, subject to fulfillment of criteria of CGPA ≥ 7.5 with no backlog and no disciplinary case. The scholarship applicable to the economically weak students based upon the income of the parents / guardian (in absence of living or working parents) subject to verification of requisite documents by competent authority of SMIT on a case-to-case basis as per availability. Required Documents: Application for special scholarship along with mandatory (any) two documents from the below-mentioned certificates need to be submitted through Chairman Admission for approval from HoI in advance: Income Certificate issued from District Magistrate (Preferred) / SDO / SDM. Income Affidavit from Notary by parents at the commencement of each semester. Income Tax Return (ITR-V “Acknowledgment Receipt”) of two preceding financial years. Scholarships are provided as per the university policy.
