                    Institute providing multiple programs-B.Tech. in 08 branches, M.Tech. in 06 branches, BCA & MCA, BBA
                    & MBA, M.Sc. in Basic Sciences & Ph.D. in Management, Science and Technology. Presently the total
                    annual student intake stands at 1200. SMIT has been selected as one of the prestigious Atal Incubation Centre funded (Grant of Rs 8
                        Crores) by NITI Ayog, GoI. The incubation centre has key thrust areas in Technology, Market
                        Affiliate, Biotechnology, ICT etc. The incubation center plans to incubate atleast 100 incubatee
                        companies in the next 5 years and nurture them to be successful business models. The incubator
                        also has a large and very experienced group of mentor board. SMIT has a consistent placement record of over 90% in the last three years. The highest package
                        offered was Rs 22 Lakhs PA by Microsoft . SMIT has successfully completed 52 sponsored projects worth Rs. 15.40 crores . Our
                        sponsors are AICTE, DST, DBT, DIETY, DRDO and ISRO and many other Govt. organizations. SMIT offers Centre of Excellence in “5G Internet of Things (IOT)” , “Healthcare Data Analytics” and “Graphene Back Surface Field Based Low Cost
                                Silicon Nanowire Solar Cells” and “Remote Sensing and Geographical
                                Information Systems (RS-GIS)”. One radar indigenously designed at SMIT has been successfully installed at DRDO, Hyderabad. Supercomputing facility – High Performance Computing facility “PARAM SHAVAK” has
                        been installed at SMIT in collaboration with C-DAC, Pune . There are 600 and more research publications in peer reviewed international journals, 36 and
                        more national journal publications, 225 international and 70 national conference publications. Two patents filed and one student patent (Ref. No: E-2/149/2017-KOL) titled “An Automated
                            Bicycle with Artificial Intelligence ” has been granted. Three Start-ups by students have been registered under Government of India. One of the Start-Up “After Glow Pvt. Ltd.” has been selected amongst the winners of Elevate 100 by Government of Karnataka in August 2017. SMU has granted Rs.83.0 Lakhs under Dr. Ramdas Pai and Mrs. Vasanthi Pai Endowment Fund to SMIT
                        during 2016-17. The same amount is approved for 2017-18. SMIT has emerged as one of the Top Private Engineering College and is placed at the 12th
                        position in " Top Private Engineering Colleges(All India) ", 2nd position in
                        " Top Private Colleges (East Zone) "and secured 7th position in " Top
                            Private Engineering Colleges Offering Best Annual Average Salar y" in India Today
                        rankings -2018. NIRF Ranking : Sikkim Manipal University of which SMIT is a constituent college is ranked in the
                        band 101-150 under the University category and in the band 151-200 under the Overall category by
                        MHRD, National Institute Ranking Framework. A Centre for Excellence in Remote Sensing & GIS has been established in Institute with the
                        support of ISRO. Institute has also been awarded the prestigious ISO 9001:2008 certification and the same has
                        been certified under Norsk Akkreditering, Norway. After the establishment of International Collaboration Cell, SMIT in 2017 two students from ME
