The Department supports and contributes to national programs like "Make in India" and "Digital India" as well as India's Semiconductor Mission and also a proud recipient of the prestigious Govt. of India sponsored 5G Use Case Lab. The Department is enriched with various state-of-the-art laboratories, including those focused on Analog and Digital Circuits, Microprocessors, VLSI and Embedded Systems, Signal Processing, Programming, Wireless and 5G Communications, etc. and also has highly experienced and qualified Faculty Members and Staff. The Department boosts of high impact and latest research activities in the areas of Semiconductors, VLSI & Nanotechnology, RADAR/Antenna/Filter Design, 5G Communication, 6G-IoT, Satellite Communication etc. with many sponsored projects from DRDO, DST, AICTE and ISRO worth INR 1.1842 Cr.
