                            over 35 acres in a picturesque valley surrounded by green hills blessed with nature’s finest
                            gifts on the banks of river Teesta. The state-of-the-art campus is the home of over 3000
                            students, over 200 faculties and a large and efficient team of support staff and the
                            international standard infrastructure is an ideal set up for quality education, healthy
                            lifestyle and all-round growth of the students. SMIT is a fully residential institute with separate hostel for boys and girls, providing a
                            home away from home to over 3000 students. There are separate students’ mess and canteen for
                            boys and girls providing vegetarian and non-vegetarian meals and a food court for all. FACILITIES All the students are provided twin sharing well furnished rooms with attached bathrooms. Limited Single (AC/Non-AC) and Double (AC) also available. Night Canteens are available in all hostels. Non-interrupted electricity and water supply is ensured throughout the year. * Single (Non AC), Double Sharing (AC) and Single (AC) shall be allotted subject to
                                availability of rooms. SMIT hostel rooms are double bedded with attached toilet, comfortably-furnished and make for
                            a good home away from home. Girls and boys have separate blocks and each block has a fixed
                            capacity. In this kind of accommodation, students have access to TV room ( Indoor for girls hostel & in
                            amphitheater for boys hostels) , Water Coolers for RO fed cold and normal drinking water and
                            other leisure/sports activities within the hostel premises. Also includes the following
                            facilities: 24 x 7 Wi-Fi connectivity provided in all hostels CCTV cameras at entrances of all hostels & at amphitheater. 24 x 7 electricity and water supply in all the hostels Night cafeterias up to 1.00 AM Solar panels installed in all hostels for hot water Hostel attenders and housekeeping services are provided in all hostels during day

                                        Grievances The safety of students, faculty members & visitors are of paramount importance to us.The
                            Home guards of Govt. of Sikkim and security guards of a private security agency are employed
                            for providing security cover to the campus. A total of 50 security guards are
                            employed to provide round the clock vigil. The Manager Administration is the Chief Security officer of the Campus. manual
                                            on security, facilities & disaster management OVERVIEW The campus though located in a hilly terrain has adequate indoor and outdoor sports
                            facilities to include Football, Badminton, Squash, Cricket, Tennis, Table tennis,
                            Volleyball, Basketball, Swimming pool, Aerobics and Gymnasium. The college has highly
                            qualified Male & Lady Sports officer always available in the campus responsible for coaching
                            and conduct of various sports events. All outdoor court/play ground has flood lights. INTER-DEPARTMENT COMPETITIONS Depending on the semester, the inter-department sports competitions are categorized as Odd

                            events are published in semester wise sports calendar. INTER-COLLEGE COMPETITIONS The college team takes part in tournaments organized by colleges/sports organizations across
                            the country. SOP on conduct of contact & games There is a 24x7 Health center in the campus having 05 Beds, 02 Resident Doctors, 03 Nurses &
